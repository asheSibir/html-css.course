Mission & Strategie
The mission of L'Oréal Group is offering all women and men worldwide the best of cosmetics innovation in terms of quality, efficacy and safety.
L'Oréal has chosen a unique strategy: Universalisation. It means globalisation that captures, understands and respects differences. Differences in desires, needs and traditions.
